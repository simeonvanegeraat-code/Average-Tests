Our Mission

At HumanAverage, we believe that understanding where you stand in the world shouldn't be complicated. We aggregate complex datasets from reputable global organizations and turn them into easy-to-understand tools and articles.

Whether you are curious about your height, your salary, or your coffee habits, our goal is to provide a neutral, data-driven perspective on human life in the 21st century.

Data Sources & Methodology

We do not make up data. All statistics presented on this website are aggregated from publicly available reports by international organizations. Our primary sources include:

  • OECD (Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development): For salary, work hours, and productivity data.
  • WHO (World Health Organization): For health, life expectancy, and BMI statistics.
  • NCD-RisC: For historical height and weight trends.
  • World Bank: For population and demographic shifts.
