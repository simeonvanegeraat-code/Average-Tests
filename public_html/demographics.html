The Great Shift: India vs. China

For decades, China was the undisputed heavyweight of population. However, in late 2023, India officially took the crown. In 2026, the gap is widening. While India's population continues to grow (driven by a young median age of 28), China is facing a demographic crisis with a shrinking workforce and a median age nearing 40.

The "Silver Tsunami"

The world is getting older. In Europe and Japan, the number of people over 65 is reaching historic highs. This impacts everything from pension systems to healthcare costs.

Conversely, Africa remains the youngest continent, with 70% of the population under the age of 30. By 2050, it is estimated that 1 in 4 humans will be African.
