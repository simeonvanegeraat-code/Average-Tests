Why is the difference so big?

Switzerland and the United States continue to dominate due to their strong banking and tech sectors. In contrast, many European nations like the Netherlands and Germany have higher tax rates, which slightly lowers the net take-home pay, despite high gross salaries.

Cost of Living Adjustment

Remember: a high salary doesn't always mean a better lifestyle. The cost of living in Zurich (Switzerland) or New York (USA) is significantly higher than in Berlin or Amsterdam. When adjusted for purchasing power parity (PPP), the gap narrows significantly.
