The "Always On" Generation

The global average for screen time has hit 6 hours and 37 minutes per day. However, looking at the chart, we see massive regional differences.

Emerging markets like South Africa, Brazil, and the Philippines lead the world, often exceeding 9 hours per day. In contrast, wealthy Asian nations like Japan have some of the lowest screen times, possibly due to an aging population and strict workplace rules banning phones.
