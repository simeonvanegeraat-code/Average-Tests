The Coffee Index

While coffee is consumed globally, Northern Europe dominates the charts. Finland holds the top spot, with the average citizen consuming nearly 12kg of coffee per year. In contrast, tea remains the dominant beverage in China and the UK.

Social Media Usage

The average internet user spends nearly 2.5 hours per day on social media platforms. The Philippines and Brazil consistently rank highest for time spent online, while Japan and Germany rank among the lowest.
